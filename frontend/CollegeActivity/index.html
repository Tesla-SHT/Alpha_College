First College Frisbee Competition

A 5v5 Frisbee Competition was held on the evening of May 14th, hosted by Alpha College. The primary objective of the frisbee competition was to welcome the upcoming 19th Asian Games in Hangzhou. The competition featured two teams, each consisting of seven members. Each team was composed of five undergraduate students(α&β against γ&δ) as well as two graduate students or teachers from the university frisbee club.
The frisbee competition followed a 5v5 format and consisted of three matches lasting 10 minutes each. To add an element of surprise and strategy to the competition, each team has 3 special effect cards, including adding an extra teammate on the field, doubling scores, etc.
After a series of highly competitive matches, Alpha-Beta joint team emerged victorious, displaying exceptional frisbee skills and coordination. They were awarded a trophy plate as recognition for their outstanding performance. All participants received official Asian Games souvenir as participation certificates, encouraging them to continue their involvement in sports.

First College Bastketball Competition

2023.4.15, We participated the first basketball competition between colleges. Our college encountered Delta College in the semi-final. With the support of our external assistance Xinyan Lu, we showed enhanced skills and dominance. Despite Delta Academy's strong coordination, the match ended in a draw, leading to an intense overtime period. Through their solid defense and potent offense, Alpha Academy eventually secured a hard-fought victory.
In the finals, we faced Gamma College in a highly anticipated showdown. Due to the absence of their foreign player, we showcased strong prowess in the first half. the exceptional performance of Haotian Shen and high intensity defense of Jingwei Xu also propelled us to a commanding lead. As the second half unfolded and Gamma Academy's foreign player returned to the game, they narrowed the score gap. However, our adaptability and resilience ultimately allowed Alpha College to regain control and emerge as the champions of the tournament!
Haotian Shen's remarkable contributions earned him the title of "MVP" . This victory not only exemplified Alpha Academy's skill and tenacity but also highlighted the principles of sportsmanship and camaraderie cherished within the undergraduate community.

College Badminton Competiton

22023 March 25, α Dongting Yuan and δ Yuheng Chang organized the first badminton competition of our four undergraduate colleges. Each college actively prepared for the competition and set up the team recently. Our team members contained Hongtian Shen, Dongting Yuan, Shengtao Hu, Shuhan Zhao， and Jingwei Xu. The rule of competition is BO3 (three pairs of double players) and in each competition, you should get 11 scores to win. Our college narrowly competed with the γ college and reach the final competition with the δ college. Without a doubt, δ college which is the most powerful college in badminton won the championship with two powerful foreign aid. At the end of the competition, α Dongting Yuan and β Yingliangping Hu start a friendly competition with the badminton master of the lab seniors. The first badminton competition ended up in the laughter and sweat of us.
